Anubavam Pudhumai () is a 1967 Indian Tamil-language comedy thriller film directed by C. V. Rajendran in his debut and written by Chitralaya Gopu from a story by Raju. The film stars R. Muthuraman and Rajasree. It was released on 2 March 1967. The film was remade in Hindi as Hulchul (1971). == Plot == == Cast == *R. Muthuraman *Rajasree *T. R. Ramachandran *Major Sundarrajan *Manorama *T. S. Balaiah *V. S. Raghavan *R. S. Manohar *Senthamarai == Production == Anubavam Pudhumai is the directorial debut of C. V. Rajendran. Cinematography was handled by P. N. Sundaram, and editing by N. M. Shankar. == Soundtrack == Soundtrack was composed by M. S. Viswanathan. == Reception == Kalki said the film was more than half-sweet.
