Artem Yuriyovych Danylyuk (; born 6 July 2001) is a Ukrainian professional footballer who plays as a left-back on loan for Bukovyna Chernivtsi. ==Career== Born in Rivne, Danylyuk is a product of the local Veres Rivne youth sportive school and the UFK-Karpaty Lviv youth sportive school system. He played for FC Karpaty in the Ukrainian Premier League Reserves, but never made his debut for the main-squad team in the Ukrainian First League.
