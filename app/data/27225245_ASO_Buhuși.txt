Asociația Sportivă Orășenească Buhuși was a Romanian football club from Buhuși, Bacău County, which played in Liga IV Bacău. The club was founded in 2007 by Toader Șteț and Adrian Păduraru, with financial support of the Buhuși City Hall and Buhuși Local Council in order to try to ensure the continuity of the football tradition in Buhuși after Textila Buhuși was dissolved, a club with tradition in romanian football.
