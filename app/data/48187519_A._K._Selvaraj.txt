A.K. Selvaraj (born 4 July 1958) is an Indian politician. He served as a Member of Parliament, represented from Tamil Nadu state in the Rajya Sabha (the upper house of Indian Parliament). Since taking charge as the Member of the Legislative Assembly for Tirupur South, he has been bringing various welfare schemes to the people. In particular, he has introduced a program called ‘Immediate Solution to Your Grievances’ to get rid of people’s problems immediately and go directly to where people are and listen to their grievances and correct them.
