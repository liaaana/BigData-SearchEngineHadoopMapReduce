Are Tronseth (born 3 September 1981) is a former Norwegian football defender. He grew up in local club Remyra IL. He moved to Stjørdals-Blink while still a youth player. In 1997 he played for Stjørdals-Blink's senior team. He later joined Rosenborg. In 2003, he was loaned out to Ham Kam, a move which was made permanent ahead of the 2004 season as the club won promotion. This year he played 14 Norwegian Premier League games. He was later loaned out from Ham Kam to Hønefoss in 2005, Fredrikstad in 2006 and Sarpsborg Sparta in 2007.
