Anne-Marie Olivier (born 1973) is a Canadian stage actress and playwright from Quebec,"Théâtre à lire: Faire l'amour, de Anne-Marie Olivier". Voir, November 26, 2015. who won the Governor General's Award for French-language drama for her play Venir au monde at the 2018 Governor General's Awards."Anne-Marie Olivier remporte le prix littéraire du Gouverneur général avec «Venir au monde»". Le Journal de Montréal, October 30, 2018. The artistic director of Théâtre Le Trident, she was named Artist of the Year for 2018 by Le Soleil and the Société Radio-Canada."Anne-Marie Olivier remporte le titre Grand Lauréat 2018".
