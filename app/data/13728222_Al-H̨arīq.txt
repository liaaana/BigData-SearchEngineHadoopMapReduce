Al-H̨arīq or Al Hareeq () is a small town in Riyadh Province, Saudi Arabia. It is located by road south of Riyadh. As of the 2004 census it had a population of 7304 people. ==Description== Al-H̨arīq is a small village located in the south of Riyadh.
