|blank_info = |blank1_name = |blank1_info = |website = |footnotes = }} Ambalahonko is a municipality (, ) in Madagascar. It belongs to the district of Ambanja, which is a part of Diana Region. According to 2001 census the population of Ambalahonko was 6,000. Only primary schooling is available in town. The majority 98% of the population are farmers. The most important crops are coffee and oranges, while other important agricultural products are cocoa and vanilla.
