The Augmentation of Benefices Act 1677 (29 Car 2 c 8) was an Act of the Parliament of England. The whole Act was repealed by section 1 of, and Part II of the Schedule to, the Statute Law (Repeals) Act 1971. ==References== *Halsbury's Statutes, Category:Acts of the Parliament of England Category:1677 in law Category:1677 in England
