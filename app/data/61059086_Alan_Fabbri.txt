Alan Fabbri (born 4 January 1979 in Bondeno) is an Italian politician. Fabbri is a member of the right-wing party Lega Nord and served as mayor of Bondeno from 2009 to 2015. He was elected Mayor of Ferrara at the 2019 local elections and took office on 11 June 2019.
