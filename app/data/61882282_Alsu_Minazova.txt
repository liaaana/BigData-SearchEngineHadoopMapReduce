Alsu Filyusovna Minazova (; born 4 July 1998) is a Russian slalom canoeist of Tatar descent, who has competed at the international level since 2014. She won two bronze medals at the ICF Canoe Slalom World Championships, earning them in 2019 (K1 team) and 2021 (C1 team). Minazova competed at the 2020 Summer Olympics in Tokyo where she started in both women's events.
