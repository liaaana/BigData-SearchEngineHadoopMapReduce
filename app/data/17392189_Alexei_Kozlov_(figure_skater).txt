Alexei Kozlov (born 8 June 1979, in Tallinn) is an Estonian former competitive figure skater. He is the 1997 Ondrej Nepela Memorial silver medalist, the 1998 Piruetten bronze medalist, and a three-time (2001, 2003 & 2004) Estonian national champion.
