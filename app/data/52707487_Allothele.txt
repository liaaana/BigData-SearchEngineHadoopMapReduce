Allothele is a genus of African spiders in the family Euagridae. It was first described by R. W. E. Tucker in 1920. ==Species== it contains five species: *Allothele australis (Purcell, 1903) – South Africa *Allothele caffer (Pocock, 1902) (type) – South Africa *Allothele malawi Coyle, 1984 – Malawi, South Africa *Allothele regnardi (Benoit, 1964) – Congo, Angola *Allothele teretis Tucker, 1920 – South Africa ==References== Category:Euagridae Category:Mygalomorphae genera Category:Spiders of Africa
