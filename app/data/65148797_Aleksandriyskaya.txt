Aleksandriyskaya () is a rural locality (a stanitsa) and the administrative centre of Aleksandriysky Selsoviet, Kizlyarsky District, Republic of Dagestan, Russia. The population was 2,437 as of 2010. There are 13 streets.Село Александрийская на карте == Geography == Aleksandriyskaya is located 42 km northeast of Kizlyar (the district's administrative centre) by road.
