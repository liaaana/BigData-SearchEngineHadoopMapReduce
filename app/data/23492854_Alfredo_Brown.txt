Alfredo Carrow Brown (1 December 1886 – 30 August 1958) was an Argentine international footballer who played as a forward. ==Early life== Brown was an Argentine of Scottish origin. Brown had four brothers who were also Argentine international players – Carlos, Eliseo, Ernesto and Jorge – as well as one cousin, Juan Domingo. Two other brothers – Diego and Tomás – were also footballers. ==Career== Brown played club football for Alumni, and international football for the Argentina national team.
