The Anglican Diocese of Ibadan SouthAnglican Communion is one of 17 within the Anglican Province of Ibadan, itself one of 14 provinces within the Church of Nigeria. The current bishop is Akintunde Popoola.Vanguard, Nigeria The first bishop of the diocese was Jacob Ajetunmobi, who was Bishop from 1999 until his retirement in 2018. ==Notes== Category:Church of Nigeria dioceses Category:Dioceses of the Province of Ibadan
