Annabel Cervantes Muñoz (born 1969 in Barcelona) is a Spanish-Catalan writer in the Catalan language. She studied Geography and History at the University of Barcelona, with a major in the environment, but she left this occupation to dedicate herself to literature. She currently lives in Castelldefels. ==Works== *L'harmònica de vidre, 1999 *L'informe del cartògraf, 2000 *Qualsevol diumenge, esports d'aventura, 2004 *Ocell de mar endins, 2007. *Celobert, 2008. *La Maledicció d'Alietzer.
