thumb|Aare Pilv Aare Pilv (born 15 April 1976) is an Estonian poet, literary scholar, translator and critician. Pilv as born in Viljandi. He is working as a researcher at Under and Tuglas Literature Centre of the Estonian Academy of Sciences. He is a member of the poetry group Erakkond ('The Group of Hermits'). He is also a member of Estonian Writers' Union and student society Veljesto. ==Works== * 1996: poetry collection "Üle" * 1998: poetry collection "Päike ehk päike" ('Sun or Sun') * 1999: poetry collection "Tema nimi on kohus" ('Their Name is Duty') * 2002: poetry collection "Nägemist" ('Goodbye') * 2007.
