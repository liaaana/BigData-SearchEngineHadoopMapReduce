Andrea Kobetić (née Penezić; born 13 November 1985) is a retired Croatian handball player who was last played for Siófok KC and the Croatia women's national handball team. She represented Croatia at the 2008 European Women's Handball Championship, where Croatia finished 6th, and Kobetić was among the top-ten goalscorers.
