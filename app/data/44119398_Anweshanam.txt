Anweshanam is a 1972 Indian Malayalam-language mystery-thriller drama film, directed by J. Sasikumar. The film stars Prem Nazir, Sharada, Kaviyoor Ponnamma and Adoor Bhasi in the lead roles. The film had musical score by M. K.
