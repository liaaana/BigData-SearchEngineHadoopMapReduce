Asociación Deportiva Fundación Logroñés was a Spanish football team based in Logroño in the autonomous community of La Rioja. Founded in 1999, it played in 3ª División - Group 16 until 2008–09 season. The club was dissolved in 2009 due to impossibility to find funds for manage the club.
