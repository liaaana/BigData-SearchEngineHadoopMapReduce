Middle-low Chuvash ( Anat Jenchi ) - are a sub-ethnos of the Chuvash. Their native area of settlement is in the northeast of Chuvashia. As a result of migrations, representatives of the middle-lower Chuvash have also settled in many other parts of the Southern Urals and Volga regions. == Culture == === Traditional dress === It is believed that the traditional clothing of the middle-lower Chuvash has preserved the most ancient look, in contrast to the other Chuvash Sub groups who were influenced by other peoples (Viryal- Finno- Ugric, Anatri- the influence of the Tatars ).
