Austrarcturellidae is a family of crustaceans belonging to the order Isopoda. Genera: * Abyssarcturella Poore & Bardsley, 1992 * Austrarcturella Poore & Bardsley, 1992 * Dolichiscus Richardson, 1913 * Pseudarcturella Tattersall, 1921 * Scyllarcturella Poore & Bardsley, 1992 ==References== Category:Isopoda
