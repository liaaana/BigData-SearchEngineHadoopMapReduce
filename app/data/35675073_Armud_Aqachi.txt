Armud Aqachi (, also Romanized as Ārmūd Āqāchī; also known as Āmūrd Āqāchī, Ārmūd Āghājī, and Ārmūrd Āghājī) is a village in Sangar Rural District, in the Central District of Faruj County, North Khorasan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 289, in 68 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Faruj County
