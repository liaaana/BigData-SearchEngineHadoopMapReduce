Armand Dupree is a direct selling company based in the United States, owned by Tupperware Brands. The brand has been known in Mexico for over 28 years, and was sold by the Fuller Cosmeticos company there. == Products == Armand Dupree products can be found in personal care, cosmetics, fragrances, skin care, and home care categories.
