Allahlu (, also Romanized as Allahlū; also known as ‘Alavī, Alyali, Lalahlū, Lalallū, Laleh Loo, and Lalehlū) is a village in Ozomdel-e Jonubi Rural District, in the Central District of Varzaqan County, East Azerbaijan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 820, in 190 families. == References == Category:Towns and villages in Varzaqan County
