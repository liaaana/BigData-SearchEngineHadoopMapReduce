Aspergillus templicola is a species of fungus in the genus Aspergillus. It is from the Flavipedes section. The species was first described in 2014.Visagie, C.M.; Hirooka, Y.; Tanney, T.; Whitfield, E.; Mwange, K.; Meijer, M.; Amend, A.S.; Seifert, K.A.; Samson, R.A. 2014.
