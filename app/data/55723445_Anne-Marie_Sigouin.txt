Anne-Marie Sigouin is a Canadian politician, who serves on Montreal City Council as representative for the Saint-Paul–Émard–Saint-Henri-Ouest district in the borough of Le Sud-Ouest."La composition du nouveau conseil municipal de Montréal". Métro, November 4, 2013. A member of the Projet Montréal political party, she was first elected in the 2013 municipal election and subsequently reelected in the 2017 election.
