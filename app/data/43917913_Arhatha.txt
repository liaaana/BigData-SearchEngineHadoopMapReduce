Arhatha is a 1990 Indian Malayalam film, directed by I. V. Sasi and produced by P. K. R. Pillai. The film stars Mohanlal, Urvashi, Rekha and Suresh Gopi in the lead roles. The film has musical score by Shyam. ==Plot== The story revolves around a chemical factory where Krishnadas and his son Mahesh rule the factory with the help of all the bad elements in society. They are corrupted politicians including Ex-minister Unnithan, police officers including the commissioner to local criminals led by the notorious Cheku.
