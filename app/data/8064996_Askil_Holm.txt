240px|thumb|Askil Holm. Askil Holm (born 1 June 1980) is a Norwegian singer and musician from Namsos. In 2006, he was a part of the big success when he played together with Espen Lind, Alejandro Fuentes and Kurt Nilsen. Their record Hallelujah Live sold 260,000 copies in Norway.
