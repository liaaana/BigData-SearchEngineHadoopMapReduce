Ajit Sharma is a businessman turned Indian politician belonging to Indian National Congress. He is a member of the Bihar Legislative Assembly. He won from Bhagalpur (Vidhan Sabha constituency) in 2014, 2015 and 2020 Bihar Legislative Assembly election.
