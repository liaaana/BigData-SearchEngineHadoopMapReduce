Antomanov (; masculine) or Antomanova (; feminine) is a Russian last name.Ganzhina, p. 12 While this last name is considered to simply be a variety of the last name Avtonomov (which is derived from a patronymic itself derived from the male first name Avtonom), it is also possible that this particular form was influenced by the first name Anton. ==References== ===Notes=== ===Sources=== *И.
