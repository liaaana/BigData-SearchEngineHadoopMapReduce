Aleksandr Alekseyevich Minayev (; 11 August 1954 – 6 December 2018) was a Russian football player and coach. ==Honours== * Soviet Top League winner: 1976 (spring). * Soviet Cup winner: 1977. * Olympic bronze: 1976. * European Under-23 Championship winner: 1976. ==International career== Minayev made his debut for USSR on 20 March 1976 in a friendly against Argentina.
