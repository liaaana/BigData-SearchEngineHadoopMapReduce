Ali Khan-e Zaman (, also Romanized as ʿAlī Khān-e Zamān; also known as ‘Alīkhān-e Zamān and Deh-e ‘Alīkhān-e Zamān) is a village in Qorqori Rural District, Qorqori District, Hirmand County, Sistan and Baluchestan Province, Iran. At the 2006 census, its population was 222, in 50 families. == References == Category:Populated places in Hirmand County
