thumb|López Muñoz in 1913 Antonio López Muñoz (1 April 1850 – 12 March 1929) was a Spanish nobleman, writer and politician who served as Minister of State in 1913, during the reign of King Alfonso XIII of Spain. ==Biography== He was born on 1 April 1850 in Huelva, Spain. In 1913 he served as Minister of State. He died on 12 March 1929 in Madrid, Spain. ==External links== *Personal dossier of the Count of López Muñoz.
