Alfonso López de Avila (died 30 December 1591) was a Roman Catholic prelate who served as the Archbishop of Santafé en Nueva Granada (1591) (in Latin) and Archbishop of Santo Domingo (1580–1591). (in Latin) ==Biography== Alfonso López de Avila was born in Córdoba, Spain. On 14 March 1580 he was appointed by the King of Spain and confirmed by Pope Gregory XIII as Archbishop of Santo Domingo.
