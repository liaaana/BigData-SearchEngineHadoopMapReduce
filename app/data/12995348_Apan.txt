The municipality covers an area of 346.9 km². ==Overview-Twin Towns-sister Cities == As of 2005, the municipality had a total population of 39,247. It was an important site in the War of Independence (1810-1821). It is a center for the production of pulque and for the sport of charrería, or Mexican rodeo .
