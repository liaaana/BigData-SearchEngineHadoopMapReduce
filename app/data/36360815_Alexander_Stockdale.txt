Alexander Stockdale (c. 1509 – 1563), of Kingston upon Hull, Yorkshire, was an English politician and landowner. ==Family== Stockdale was married to Grace née Estofte, and they had a son and a daughter. ==Career== Stockdale was Mayor of Kingston upon Hull from 1544 to 1545, 1551 to 1552, and 1558 to 1559.
