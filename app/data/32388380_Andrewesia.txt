Andrewesia is a genus in the beetle family Carabidae. There are at least three described species in Andrewesia. ==Species== These three species belong to the genus Andrewesia: * Andrewesia apicalis (Chaudoir, 1872) (Southeast Asia) * Andrewesia australica Baehr, 2009 (Australia) * Andrewesia obesa (Andrewes, 1923) (Indomalaya) ==References== Category:Lebiinae
